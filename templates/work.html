About the idea

An interactive map where users can search for specific terms and see what the world is talking about

We wanted to show how beautiful communication and information can look. An interactive map which shows where people talk a lot about certain topics. You just need to type in your favourite interest, celebrity and our map takes you to real hot areas. (No, Ryan Gosling won't appear, but you might look for places where people tweet about him a lot.)

Of course you might say certain topics are definitely more popular in various cities and countries, but then again, you never know
